Skeletal Muscle Mass (SSM)

Skeletal muscles are one of the most impressive parts of the human body - Thousands of individual fibers contracting and relaxing every single day to get us from one place to the next. 1, 2 The average SSM of a healthy adult comprises a considerable 30% to 40% of total body mass. 5

The fibers of skeletal muscles are patterned with red and white lines called striations that give them a distinct look. Unlike Lean Body Mass (LBM) and Lean Muscle Mass (LMM), SSM is only the muscle tissue that can be grown through an exercise stimulus. 3 The name "skeletal muscle" is in reference to these muscles attaching to bones via tendons. 4, 1
