      Skeletal Muscle Mass (SSM)

      Skeletal muscles are one of the most impressive parts of the human body. They're more than just the muscles we work in the gym — they contract tens of thousands of times a day to produce all of our movements. 1 , 2 The average SSM of a healthy adult comprises about 30–40% of total body mass. 5

      Skeletal muscle fibers show striations that give them a distinct look. Unlike Lean Body Mass (LBM) or Lean Muscle Mass (LMM), SSM is the muscle tissue that can be grown through exercise. 3 The name "skeletal muscle" refers to how they attach to bones via tendons. 4
